### Long Description (Page Bio)
```
Brandea is a business dashboard built specifically for content creators and micro-influencers.

Track every brand deal from first DM to final payment. Generate PDF invoices with VAT in 60 seconds. See your earnings, pipeline, and upcoming deadlines — all in one view.

No more spreadsheets. No more missed payments. No more "wait, did they pay me?"

Features:
- 7-stage deal pipeline (Kanban board)
- PDF invoicing with VAT compliance
- Auto payment reminders (email + WhatsApp)
- Revenue analytics by platform
- Content calendar linked to deals
- Brand payment reliability ratings
- Mobile-first design

Free for up to 5 deals. No credit card required.

Built by creators, for creators.
```
