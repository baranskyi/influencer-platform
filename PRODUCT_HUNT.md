# Product Hunt Launch

## Name
Brandea

## Tagline
Deal tracker & invoicing built for content creators

## Description
Brandea is a business dashboard built specifically for content creators and micro-influencers. Track every brand deal from first DM to final payment. Generate PDF invoices in 60 seconds. See your earnings, pipeline, and upcoming deadlines in one view.

No more spreadsheets, no more missed payments, no more "wait, did they pay me?"

Unlike HoneyBook or Bonsai, Brandea is laser-focused on creator workflows — lightweight, fast, and free for up to 5 deals. Built by a creator who got tired of juggling Notion, Google Sheets, and email.

## First Comment

Hey Product Hunt!

I built Brandea because I was tired of watching creator friends lose money to disorganization. They'd forget to invoice a brand, miss a payment deadline, or have no idea how much they actually earned last month — all while juggling DMs, emails, and spreadsheets.

The tools that exist (HoneyBook, Bonsai) are built for freelancers and agencies — they're bloated, expensive, and don't speak "creator." Notion is flexible but requires hours of setup and zero automation.

So I built something simple: a clean dashboard where you track deals from pitch to payment, generate invoices in one click, and actually see your business at a glance.

What makes Brandea different:
- Built specifically for influencers and content creators
- Free for up to 5 deals (no credit card, no trial expiration)
- PDF invoices in 60 seconds
- Fully responsive — works from your phone between shoots
- No learning curve — if you can use Instagram, you can use Brandea

I'd love your feedback — what features would make this essential for your workflow?
